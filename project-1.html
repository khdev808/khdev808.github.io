Dimple

Dimple reimagines dating with real-time video, stories, and icebreakers—built with Swift, WebRTC, Firebase, and a whole lot of heart. Meet friends or spark something new, face to face.

Live Link

Project Overview

Date. Make Friends. LiveMatch. Dimple is a modern dating app built for real connection—with real-time video, engaging stories, and features that actually help people spark conversations, not just swipe endlessly. We collaborated with the Dimple team to bring their vision to life, developing the mobile app with Swift, Node.js, Firebase, WebRTC, Socket.IO, VoxImplant, AWS, Realm, Google Maps, MessageKit, and AI-enhanced features. The result is an app that feels less like an algorithm—and more like a vibe.

Key Features

  • LiveMatch: Meet new people in real time with a dating experience that feels almost IRL.
  • Swipe & Schedule: Match, message, video chat, or set up a date on your own timeline.
  • Stories: Share your day with 24-hour photo and video updates that invite interaction.
  • Flavors: Conversation starters that make first messages less awkward and more fun.
  • Unlimited Likes: Because no one should run out of ways to show interest.

From user onboarding to live video integration, Dimple rethinks what dating apps can be—personal, playful, and built with intention.
