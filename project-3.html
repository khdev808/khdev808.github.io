Partnered with the CheckSammy team to build their mobile app with React Native and Node. Designed to support smarter recycling and track ESG impact—sustainability at scale.

Project Overview

Sustainability as a Service—backed by data, scale, and tech. We worked closely with the CheckSammy team to build their mobile app from the ground up using React Native, Node.js, and Google APIs, creating a seamless experience for both haulers and clients. CheckSammy is a leading sustainability company with a nationwide network of 25,000+ recycling and reverse logistics facilities and 5,000+ haulers. Their app, powered by Veridiant, delivers actionable, auditable data to help businesses improve ESG performance, track diversion scores, and make smarter, greener decisions.

Key Features

    • Real-time logistics tools for haulers and recycling operations
    • Integrated data tracking to support ESG and sustainability goals
    • Custom routing and geolocation powered by Google APIs
    • Scalable architecture built to support a fast-growing nationwide network

    The result? A powerful mobile platform helping companies turn sustainability into a measurable, strategic advantage.
